About Me

Hey — I'm Zamil, 17, from Bangladesh 🇧🇩.

I love gaming way more than I love studying, and honestly that math isn't even close. You'll usually catch me deep in Minecraft, grinding Roblox, pulling on banners in Genshin Impact, or fighting bosses in Terraria. When I'm not gaming, I'm editing videos and montages for my YouTube channel — it's the kind of "fun" that somehow eats six hours without asking.

Music is basically my life support: Spotify runs 24/7 in my ears. School? Yeah… not my favorite arc, but I'm surviving it one skipped homework at a time. Also — and this is important — I hate fish. 🐟🚫 That's the whole personality. Just a chill guy vibing through life, playing games, making edits, and pretending deadlines are a myth.
